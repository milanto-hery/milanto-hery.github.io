About Me

I am Milanto, a data scientist and machine learning researcher with a solid foundation in mathematics, physics, and computational modeling. My passion is building intelligent systems that extract meaningful insights from complex data to support real-world decision-making.

I have experience across the full data science pipeline — from raw data acquisition and preprocessing to model development, evaluation, and deployment. I prioritize creating models that are robust, interpretable, and grounded in the realities of the data.

My research focuses on environmental monitoring, wildlife conservation, and ecological analysis, including bioacoustics, computer vision, and remote sensing, where automated analysis of large-scale datasets is essential.

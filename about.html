About Me

I am Milanto, a data scientist and machine learning researcher with a strong foundation in applied mathematics, physics, and computational modeling. I design intelligent systems that extract meaningful insights from complex data to inform real-world decisions.

My experience spans the full data pipeline: data acquisition, preprocessing, model development, evaluation, and deployment. I emphasize robust data representation and interpretable machine learning models.

My research is interdisciplinary, focusing on AI applications in environmental monitoring, wildlife conservation, and ecological analysis, including bioacoustics, computer vision, and remote sensing for large-scale automated analysis.

About Me

I am Milanto, a data scientist and machine learning researcher with a strong foundation in applied mathematics, physics, and computational modeling. I build intelligent systems to extract meaningful information from complex data, supporting decision-making in real-world contexts.

I have experience across the full data science pipeline: raw data acquisition, preprocessing, model development, evaluation, and deployment. I focus on designing robust representations and interpretable, effective models.

My interdisciplinary research applies AI to environmental monitoring, wildlife conservation, and ecological analysis. This includes work in bioacoustics, computer vision, and remote sensing for large-scale automated data analysis.

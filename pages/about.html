About Me

I am Milanto, a data scientist and machine learning researcher with a strong foundation in applied mathematics, physics, and computational modeling. My work focuses on creating intelligent systems that extract meaningful insights from complex data to support decision-making.

I have experience across the full data science pipeline — from raw data acquisition and preprocessing, to model development, evaluation, and deployment. I prioritize robust, interpretable models grounded in the structure and limitations of data.

My research interests are interdisciplinary, particularly in AI applications for environmental monitoring, wildlife conservation, and ecological analysis, including bioacoustics, computer vision, and remote sensing.

About Me

I am Milanto Ferdinand Rasolofohery, a data scientist and machine learning researcher with a strong foundation in applied mathematics, physics, and computational modeling. My work focuses on building intelligent systems that extract meaningful information from complex data and support decision-making in real-world contexts.

I have worked across the entire data science and ML pipeline: data acquisition, preprocessing, model development, evaluation, and deployment. I focus on understanding data structure, designing robust representations, and building interpretable models.

My research is highly interdisciplinary. I am motivated by applications of AI to environmental monitoring, wildlife conservation, and ecological analysis. I work with bioacoustics, computer vision, and remote sensing to automate large-scale data interpretation.

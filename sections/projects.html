My Work

I focus on designing ML systems grounded in a deep understanding of both the data and the domain. I build pipelines that integrate signal processing, statistical reasoning, and modern deep learning techniques.

I have extensive experience with high-dimensional data: audio signals, spectrograms, images, and video. This includes preprocessing, representation selection, and evaluation with meaningful metrics. I aim for scalable systems capable of reliable real-world performance.

Communication and reproducibility are crucial. My work is well-documented, transparent, and accessible to technical and interdisciplinary collaborators alike.
